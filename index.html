Cooking is the process of making food through the application of heat. The purpose of cooking and eating is to provide your body with the necessary nutrients it needs for survival.

Animals have a connective tissue called collagen which makes it chewy. Heat and time is needed to break down that collagen thus making it tender. The amount of muscle an animal has is determined by how frequently they use that muscle.

Types of Cooking

The ways you can cook food is dependent on two factors.

The first is the type of heat, dry or wet.

The second is the duration of cooking, fast or slow.
